Virtual Pet

First part of a three stage project. Created a virtual pet similar to the orignal one for kids back in the 1990s. The virtual pet is named Thule. Thule will be able to inform you when she needs various tasks to be completed. For example, when Thule is either hungry or thirsty. It is up to the Owner of Thule to keep their virtual pet happy and Alive .

In this iteration of Virtual Pet Shelter, the Virtual Shelter that was made in part two, now has Dogs, Cats and Robot Dogs and Cats. The user will nee to manage the pets hunger, thirst, boredom, waste, happiness, oil levels. Along with cleaning out the Shelter litter box.
